# Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics
Transforming raw data into business intelligence using Advanced Excel. I developed dynamic reports to analyze sales performance and financial health. Sales: Identified customers and regional trends to optimize revenue.  Finance: Created P&amp;L statements and Target vs. Actual reports.  Stack: Power Query, Power Pivot, DAX, and Interactive Dashboards.
## Sales Report :


- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create a _[customer performance report](https://github.com/Deepak300899/Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)

    **2.** Conduct a comprehensive comparison between _[market performance and sales targets](https://github.com/Deepak300899/Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target%20Report.pdf)

- **Purpose of sales analytics:** Enables businesses to monitor activities, evaluate performance, and identify key sales patterns and KPIs.

- **Importance of analyzing sales data:** Identify sales patterns and track key performance indicators (KPIs).

- **Role of reports:** These reports help determine optimal discounts, strengthen consumer negotiations, and pinpoint global expansion opportunities.


## Finance Report :

- **Project objective:** 

    **1.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Fiscal Year](https://github.com/Deepak300899/Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf) & _[Months](https://github.com/Deepak300899/Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)


   **2.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by _[Markets](https://github.com/Deepak300899/Excel-Sales-and-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf)

- **Purpose of sales analytics:** Facilitates financial evaluation, strategic decision-making, and clear stakeholder communication.

- **Importance of analyzing Finance data:** Aid in benchmarking against industry peers and previous periods Foundation for budgeting and forecasting.

- **Role of reports:** Supports industry benchmarking, precise budgeting, and forecasting to align financial outlooks with long-term goals.
